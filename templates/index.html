Pre-requisites

  • A LinkedIn account.
  • More than 500 connections (For best results).
  • Account on rapidapi.

Tutorial

  • Visit this link.
  • Subscibe to Basic plan (Free). You are free to choose any plan.
  • On Bottom-Left of the screen. Click any link under Profile APIs.
  • Go to Right side of the screen, under Code Snippets.
  • You will see something like "x-rapidapi-key": "GIBBERISH TEXT"
  • Copy the GIBBERISH TEXT (Excluding inverted commas ""). Paste it in Your API Key

Read before you proceed!

  • Turn off two factor authentication of your LinkedIn account
  • LinkedIn is strict about scraping and automation. Don't get too aggresive while using this application, it may block your account.
  • We don't save your personal information (including your password and api key).
  • Basic plan comes with 50 requests/month. That means, you can collect very limited profiles. So be sure with your search.
  • With basic plan, you can come once every month to collect LinkedIn profiles.
  • Once the process of data collection is complete. A csv file will be download in your machine.
  • Note:Emails in the csv are not the official emails but some predictions of a well-trained AI model.
